Actuarial Field Manual
==================================================

This guide serves to bridge the gap between actuarial theory and its practical application in the workplace. It is intended to help students and practitioners apply what they have learned in the standard exam curriculum by providing examples in commonly-used open source technologies, such as Python, R, and SQL.
